TalentNest HR — Recruitment Platform, Job Board & HRMS Software India

TalentNest HR is India's end-to-end hiring and HR management platform. Recruiters manage their pipeline from first application to signed offer. HR admins run analytics, pre-boarding, and document verification. Candidates browse jobs, track every application live, and sign offer letters digitally — all in one place.

Three Products. One Platform.

  • HireBoard — Recruitment pipeline for talent teams. Manage candidates, schedule interviews, extend offers, and track every stage from Applied to Hired.
  • PeopleDesk — HR management for admins and org managers. Analytics, job posting, pre-boarding, background verification, offer letters, and team management.
  • JobTrack — Job search and application tracking for candidates. Browse jobs, see your AI match score, track every application live, join video interviews, and sign your offer from your phone.

Staffing Services

  • IT Staffing — Software engineers, cloud architects, DevOps, data scientists
  • Cybersecurity Staffing — SOC analysts, penetration testers, GRC specialists
  • Non-IT Staffing — Finance, HR, sales, and executive search
  • Contract to Hire (C2H) — Trial periods with full payroll administration
  • Corp to Corp (C2C) — B2B contractor placements
  • Permanent Staffing — Direct placement with 90-day replacement guarantee
  • HRMS Platform — Smart attendance, shift management, payroll inputs, compliance

Why TalentNest HR

Unlike traditional job boards, TalentNest HR connects the entire hiring chain — the recruiter managing the pipeline, the HR admin running the org, and the candidate tracking their application — in one platform. Built for Indian companies. Based in Hyderabad, Telangana.
