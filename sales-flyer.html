Platform Capabilities
📊
Analytics & Insights
Total Applications · Total Candidates · Total Offers · Total Placements · Fill Rate · Application Velocity · Top Performance Jobs. Click any data point to view the candidates behind it.
⚡
Workflow Automations
Set trigger + action rules: when a candidate reaches any stage, automatically send a branded email or WhatsApp message — no manual follow-up needed.
📄
Offer Letter Engine
Generate branded offer letters with custom CTC, joining date, benefits, and clauses. Send by email with CC, track status (Draft → Sent → Signed), and allow digital acceptance from the candidate portal.
🎥
Built-In Video Interviews
No Zoom link-sharing or third-party setup. Schedule and join interviews inside the platform. Interviewers complete structured scorecards — strengths, areas for improvement, overall rating — immediately after.
📝
Skill Assessments
Build custom question banks per role. Send assessments to candidates in-platform. Review scores, strengths, and improvement areas without leaving the pipeline.
🔔
Multi-Channel Notifications
Every key event triggers alerts via in-app bell notifications, email, WhatsApp, and push notifications — keeping recruiters, candidates, clients, and hiring managers aligned in real time.
🌐
Branded Career Page
Each organisation gets a public career page with custom branding — logo, colours, header text, and support email. SEO-optimised job listings appear instantly when a job is published.
✅
Pre-Boarding & BGV
Auto-create pre-boarding checklists the moment a candidate is marked Hired. Track document collection, admin review, and BGV status — all in one place. Never chase a new joiner over email again.
🏊
Talent Pool
Park strong candidates who aren't right for the current role. When a matching position opens up, recruiters filter the talent pool by CTC, experience, skills, and location — and re-engage in seconds.
📬
Outreach & Interest Tracking
Send interest emails to pooled candidates. Track who responded as Interested or Not Interested. See interest rates per campaign — so your outreach actually converts.
📧
Custom Email Engine
Bring your own SMTP or use platform email. Set a custom From Name, From Email, email signature, and footer. Every automated notification goes out under your brand.
🔒
Security & Audit
Full audit log for every action across the platform. Role-based access control: super_admin, admin, recruiter, client, hiring manager, and candidate — each scoped precisely.
